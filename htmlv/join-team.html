Join The Team

Working for Initi8 is a great option for anyone, whether you’re looking to get into recruitment for the first time, or you’re an experienced consultant who wants to work for a company where you are valued and developed for more than just your billings!

We’re always looking out for fresh talent to join the team, so if you are interested, get in touch with us.

Why join initi8?

Exciting Roles

No two days are the same at Initi8, which keeps us on our toes. We recruit nationally and internationally and use cutting edge recruitment technology.

Working as a recruitment consultant you will have a varied job where two days are never the same.You will recruit both nationally and internationally, getting the chance to speak to the people that are shaping and building the digital world.

As a Consultant at Initi8, you will work with a vastly experienced team who have cultivated relationships with amazing clients in the last decade. You will get the chance to learn from some of the best in our industry on a level playing field. You will use the very best RecTech available, including Video, ML/AI tools and digital marketing solutions, meaning you will have a huge advantage over any competitor.

Comprehensive Training

Our world class training is extensive and continuous, to ensure we deliver the very best to our clients and candidates.

Our Junior Consultants complete a 13 week induction & training course, which covers every element of the job. We also offer a course for our Senior and Principle Consultants, to ensure best practice.

All our team members, regardless of their role, complete an extensive diversity and inclusion training package on starting at Initi8.

We also engage regularly with an external trainer who we believe is one of the best IT recruitment trainers in the world!

Central London Location

Our office is based behind London’s iconic Southbank.

Our office is a short walk from Waterloo, London Bridge and Blackfriars tube stations. It is equipped with bike storage, an onsite barista and is also dog friendly!

Our central location means there is plenty to do. Lunch breaks can be spent at London Bridge Market, by the river or at the Pure Gym round the corner!

Fantastic Perks

We believe in working hard and playing hard, so we offer an excellent range of perks.

As well as being part of a supportive and positive team, we offer the following perks:

  • An industry beating commission on top of a competitive basic salary
  • An annual bonus
  • 20 days of holiday, which increased to 30 over time
  • Access to a life and wellbeing coach, as mental wellbeing is crucial to us at Initi8
  • Gym memberships are provided and use of the gym is encouraged in line with our focus on health
  • Team socials including regular nights out, and monthly lunch clubs to restaurants such as The Ivy and Sushi Samba, for high performers
  • An individual incentive scheme which can range from shopping trips to weekends away
  • A team incentive scheme, which usually involve a weekend away in Ibiza!
