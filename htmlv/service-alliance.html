Full Service Alliance

Our Full Service Alliance service brings your team together with ours, creating an embedded talent solution.

READ MORE

By combining our team together with yours, we create an embedded talent team that can plan and build strategic recruitment pipelines. It can help you with employer branding and add specific technical expertise as and where it is needed.

It’s our full service solution. There are no gaps in the process. If you’re looking to hire a lot of people quickly or you have consistent requirements, this product is for you. It flips the traditional recruitment model on its head and enables you to have a fully trained and expert in-house recruitment function. Using cutting edge recruitment technology and members of our team, we can source immediate hires and longer terms strategic hires in unison with your organisation. 

You receive access to our full suite of technology, including a video interviewing platform, AI and ML search tools, an enormous network and many years of industry specific experience.

You will pay a monthly fixed fee per consultant. Each consultant will fill on average 3-4 professionals per month. This will give you the upper hand in your quest to find, attract and hire the best global talent!
